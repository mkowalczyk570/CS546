My travel destinations

Runeterra

Oh Runeterra. What a wild place to visit. An ancient land of magic, dragons, yordles, living mountains and so many more. Primordial magic built this place, and many legends hint that primordial magic will also destroy it. If there is any reason to go, it'd be for the sheer wonder and excitement you will experience when met face to face with some of the creatures that roam. You may even come into contact with some wild, unchained magic, which will be one hell of an experience.

Middle-Earth

A place such as this is a sight to behold. From the The Shire to Mount Doom, and all else in between, what a phenomenal adventure it was to travel these lands. Along the way are many incredible sights to see, and all the tales of hobbits, dwarves, and man that are told, it is an unforgettable experience.

  2. Rivendell
  3. Dol Guldur
  4. Mount Doom
